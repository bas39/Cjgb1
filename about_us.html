Back in 2008, when the recession began, two business men saw an emerging market - precious metal purchasing, or as the public knows it: Gold buying shops.

 

During that time, we have changed locations, and grown with the times. Initially the idea for the store was to change into a jewelry location, with the intention to grow from there. Instead, we have moved with the times and continued to compete in the vast market of precious metals.

 

In 2014, the idea of moving into the market of Gift card purchasing became an a goal for us. Today, we are one of the many shops that provides this service - seeing as many people accumulate a vast, large amount of gift cards - the thought of cash instead of plastic makes many people interested, and seek out Central Jersey Gold Buyers for their ultimate one-stop-shop location!
